JARED CAPE 

At Dartmouth College, I studied Public Policy, Theater, and Human Geography. I am privileged to have interned for my member of Congress, produced policy memos and reports for US State, US Federal, and South African governments, and perform region planning work for NH's North Country. To cap my final year at college, I combined my interest in electoral methods and redistricting in my thesis titled “New Hampshire’s Electoral Geographies: District Types, Electoral Methods, and Political Representation.” Moving forward, I look to serve others by assisting in the research, development, and implementation of sound, data driven public policy.
